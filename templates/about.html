about me

I'm a 35 year old guy living in Co. Kildare. I like to do lots of sports and outdoor activities. I play gaelic football, canoe polo and have done a few triathlons. I also like to run. I represented Ireland for a number of years in canoe polo and played in 3 European Championships and 1 World Championship. I am also an international standard referee. I have raced in the Swiss 70.3 and the Lanzarote Ironman.
